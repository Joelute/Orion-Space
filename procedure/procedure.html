Launch Procedure:

1. Integration: stacking the rocket stages and the payload together
2. Rollout: moving the rocket to the launch pad
2. Fueling
3. Pre-launch checks
4. Starting up the rocket: switching on its internal power sources, starting the rocket's control systems etc.
5. Starting the engines and checking if they work properly
6. Launch



Safety Procedure:

In case of emergencies, we have safety procedures put in place for the safety of everyone. Our top priority is providing a safe environment and to get the people to space and back. The safety procedure is to contact the Orion Space headquarters for instructions and report any safety issues or health hazards to us.
